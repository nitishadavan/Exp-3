# Exp - 3 RAILWAY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

# AIM:
Provide an integrated system to manage train schedules, reservations, ticketing, crew & asset maintenance, real-time status, and reporting.

# SRS (Procedure):
1.User & role management — register passengers, staff, and admins with authentication and access control.

2.Train & schedule module — create routes, timetables, stoppages, and dynamic timetable updates.

3.Reservation & seat allocation — search trains, hold/confirm seats, generate PNRs and waitlist logic.

4.Payment & cancellation — integrate payment gateway, refunds, cancellation rules and receipts.

5.Real-time operations — live train status, platform assignment, GPS tracking, delays and notification system.

6.Crew, coach & maintenance management — assign crew, track coach availability, schedule inspections and repairs.

7.Reporting & security — fares/revenue reports, audit logs, backups, role-based auditing and data encryption.
